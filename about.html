About Me

Some Answers To Your Questions

My Best Work:

My best HTML/web content is hosted on Github Pages: http://glenwperry.github.io. It also includes professional writing samples.

What I’m Looking For:

The opportunity to continue my career in a place where I can grow. Over time I'd like to increase my technical and leadership knowledge while contributing to creative content. I’m willing to start on a temp/contract basis and can start with as little as two weeks notice.

A Skill I’ve Learned:

I’ve been writing creatively as a hobby all my life and it’s something I’m always trying to get better at. My main interest is in literary fiction (Lorrie Moore, Updike) and I’ve put a lot of time into honing my skills in this area. I'm half-way through completing my first novel, titled "Bad Times." I'm also always working to increase my technical/professional skills; most recently by taking courses with EdX online in the subjects of Excel for Data Analysis and Visualization, Leadership and HTML5. .

What You’re Looking For:

An employer looking for someone who cares about achieving goals. Someone who will invest themselves in the product and make it a personal point to see that it succeeds. The most successful teams need to offer something that a larger or deeper-pocketed competitor might fumble. I have an exceptional track record as a team-player and beleive it is important for colleagues to inspire each other to meet their full potential.

How I’ve Invested In Myself:

I created this website showcasing my abilities specifically for the purpose of my current job search. I signed up for FreeCodeCamp (which I found on Github) and have already completed the HTML/CSS/Bootstrap/jQuery curriculum (so far so good). I've taken online courses in Excel, Leadership and HTML5. I follow political and economic trends through the Library of Liberty and Economics as well as Democracy At Work. I also volunteer with Habitat Works, the L.A. Bike Coalition and 826LA.

What Drives Me:

My passion is to understand people, including myself. I do this through writing, through physical activity (backpacking, cycling), and by keeping a healthy desire to learn about anything and everything. I’m interested in all the secret human gunk that makes a life. I’m passionate about the aesthetic experiences, cultural tides and financial mechanisms that form our world on a personal and global scale.

If We Had a Band:

My role in the band would be pipe organist and we would play baroque horror punk. If I were to injure my fingers I would tell the rest of the band that they need to temporarily find another pipe organist but that I’m already teaching myself the theremin and that I’d still love to come to the gig up in New Brunswick.

Why These Questions:

I'm trying to give my future employer a feel for my personality across a broader spectrum than "where do you see yourself in ten years?" provides. I want to find a position in which I am a good fit, and which is a good fit for me, and I feel this is a better way of achieving it than providing a template cover letter using lots of Linkedin buzzwords.
